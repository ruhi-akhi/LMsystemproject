# Smart Inventory - Asset & Licensing Checklist

This document tracks all external assets utilized in the **Smart Inventory** template, ensuring proper licensing and compliance with ThemeForest standards.

---

## 🔡 Typography & Fonts
All fonts used are open-source and free for commercial and personal use:
* **Inter**: Distributed under the [SIL Open Font License v1.1](http://scripts.sil.org/OFL).
* **Geist & Geist Mono**: Open-source fonts developed by Vercel, licensed under the [SIL Open Font License v1.1](http://scripts.sil.org/OFL).
* **Hind Siliguri**: Open-source Bengali font distributed under the [SIL Open Font License v1.1](http://scripts.sil.org/OFL).

All fonts are dynamically served via the `@next/font` package to optimize load performance and ensure offline compatibility.

---

## 🎨 Iconography
All icons are open-source and free for commercial use:
* **Lucide React / Lucide Icons**: Licensed under the [ISC License](https://github.com/lucide-react/lucide/blob/main/LICENSE).
* **React Icons**: Wrapper library for various open-source icon sets (Material Design Icons, Font Awesome, etc.) all used under their respective permissive open-source licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0).

---

## 🖼️ Media & Photography
Any photographic assets, backgrounds, or default placeholder images are royalty-free and safe for distribution:
* **Unsplash**: All stock food and product photography are under the [Unsplash License](https://unsplash.com/license), which permits free commercial and non-commercial use without attribution.
* **pravatar.cc**: Avatar placeholders used in demonstration pages are public domain.
